[1:00 — ARCHITECTURE]
(show assets/architecture_diagram.png)
Let's take a look at the system architecture. (pause)
Our workflow begins at the START node, which routes directly into the Security Checkpoint. The checkpoint automatically redacts PII like credit cards and SSNs, detects prompt injection attempts, and filters out spam keywords.
If it passes, it moves to the CRM Orchestrator agent. (pause)
The Orchestrator coordinates the rest of the flow by delegating to two sub-agents: the Lead Scoring Agent, which checks the customer database to evaluate and tier the lead, and the Email Drafting Agent, which fetches pre-approved email templates to draft a reply.
The Orchestrator then updates the global workflow state and routes to the Human Review Node. (pause)
For High Value leads, the Human Review Node triggers a Human-in-the-Loop approval step, prompting the manager to approve or reject the response.
Finally, approved drafts resolve at the Final Output node, while rejections go to the Rejection Node.
